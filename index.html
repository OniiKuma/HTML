Starbase 13



Space Station Launch Center

To launch a mission from Starbase 13 as the Space Ranger on duty, you will need to first push the button on the control panel. THis will initiate the sequence that will lead to the rocket take off. Once the button is pushed, you will need to wait for the countdown to finish before the rocket takes off.
